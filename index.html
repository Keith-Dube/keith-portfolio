About Me

I'm Keith, a final-year Computer Science student with a strong passion for building impactful digital solutions. My interests span across web development, software engineering, data-driven problem-solving, and understanding how modern systems are designed and translated from high-level concepts to working software.

Over the years, I’ve worked on a range of projects — from academic systems like the Makhanda SPCA Management System to hands-on web applications — each one strengthening my skills in front-end and back-end development. I love taking ideas from scratch, shaping them into clean, usable interfaces, and architecting systems that make sense under the hood.

When I’m not coding, I’m sharpening my logic through martial arts, engaging with my faith community, or mentoring students in Computer Science and Statistics. I’m always learning, always improving, and driven by the goal of becoming a well-rounded developer who can contribute meaningfully to the tech industry.
